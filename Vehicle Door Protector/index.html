Methodology

The following steps were following to implement the methodology of this study:

The methodology of experimental model is based on the following steps:

  • Step 1: Power the device via USB
  • Step 2: Wait until the device is connected to the network and start streaming.
  • Step 3: Configure the video streaming through the video stream server.
  • Step 4: Run the necessary scripts for the tasks.

Use case of experimental model

  1. Record video footages
  2. Stream video footages
  3. Detect suspicious people (Face recognition)
  4. Protection for the property (Smart door lock)

Objectives

  • Developing face recognition AI Model
  • Developing experimental SMART device
  • Storing and recording video
  • Algorithm development to achieve the requirements
