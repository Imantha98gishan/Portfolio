Water Bill Manager

Introduction

Modern day people are really busy and they may not be able to manage their day to day tasks. Managing the cost of live is another vital aspect that we needed to be focused to increase the economic of the life. Today we spend money for water, electricity, internet etc. Therefore with the busy life we may not be able to keep track of them and keeping log of documents for this task could be space consume and time consume. Managing cost for water is another important scenerio same as above. Therefore in this project we are focusing to developing solution to help managing the water bill, keep log of cost for water, analytics of previous consumption and user database. This solution was developed using .netframework and SQL database.
